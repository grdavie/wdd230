WDD 230: Chamber of Commerce Site Plan

Site Name

"Ipswich Region Chamber of Commerce"

This name represents the region in Queensland Australia where the chamber operates.

Site Purpose

The Ipswich Region Chamber of Commerce is the leading organisation promoting and enhancing business and community development in the Ipswich region. This site is updated regularly and includes information of all upcoming Chamber events, a directory of local business and organisations who belong to the chamber, a discover page containing history, demographics, current events, and relevant local and national media items.

Scenarios

The website has a diverse target audience that includes: businesses, community members, government entities, investors and developers, education institutions, tourists, and job seekers. Scenarios or questions that can be asked by a site visitor may include, but not limited to:

  1. What community events and development projects are currently supported by the chamber?
  2. How can I connect with local businesses through the Chamber to sponsor or participate in community events and initiatives?
  3. Who are the board of directors and how do I contact the Chamber?
  4. How do I become a member or how do I join the Chamber?
