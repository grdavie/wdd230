Become a Member of the Ipswich Chamber of Commerce

Our mission is to strengthen and advance business in the region

The NP Membership is designed for non-profit organizations and provides basic benefits without any membership fee. If you're looking for an entry-level membership, the Bronze level offers increased exposure and access to events and training.

For a popular choice with enhanced benefits, including more special events and advanced advertising, consider the Silver membership. Finally, the Gold membership is the premium level that comes with extensive benefits, VIP access, and premium advertising opportunities.

Complete the application form to become a part of our Chamber and enjoy all of our member benefits from Day 1!
